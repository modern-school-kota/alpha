Our Leadership

Dr. Deepak Singh
Chairman
Modern School

Baran, Bharatpur, Jaipur, Kota

❝

Education is not just about academic excellence; it's about shaping character, instilling values, and preparing students to face the challenges of life with confidence and integrity. At Modern School, we strive to create an environment where every child can discover their potential and develop into responsible global citizens.

Our vision extends beyond the classroom walls, encompassing holistic development that balances intellectual growth with emotional intelligence, creativity with discipline, and tradition with innovation. We believe in nurturing young minds to become leaders who will make a positive impact on society.

Mr. Vaibhav Singh
Director
Modern School

Baran, Bharatpur, Jaipur, Kota

❝

As the Director of Modern School, my mission is to ensure that our institution remains at the forefront of educational excellence while staying true to our core values. We are committed to providing a learning environment that is both challenging and supportive, where students are encouraged to question, explore, and innovate.

Our approach combines the best of traditional teaching methodologies with modern pedagogical techniques, creating a dynamic educational experience that prepares students for the complexities of the 21st century. We take pride in our diverse community of learners and educators who together create a vibrant tapestry of knowledge, culture, and shared growth.
